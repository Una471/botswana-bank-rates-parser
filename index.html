Frequently Asked Questions

How often are the rates updated?

Our system automatically fetches the latest rates from banks every 5 minutes. This ensures you always see the most current information available.

Is this service really free?

Yes! Our comparison tool is 100% free to use. We don't charge users any fees and we're not paid commissions by banks. Our goal is to help you make the best financial decisions.

Which banks do you compare?

We currently compare rates from FNB Botswana, Stanbic Bank Botswana, Access Bank Botswana, and we're continuously adding more financial institutions.

How accurate is the savings calculator?

The calculator uses the actual current interest rates from banks to provide accurate estimates. However, actual earnings may vary based on the specific terms and conditions of each bank.

Can I apply for accounts through this site?

While we don't process applications directly, each bank listing includes direct links to their official websites and contact information where you can apply.
